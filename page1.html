The Science Behind Traffic Jams.

What are the main reasons that create traffic congestions?
There are several reasons that cause traffic jams, but only three issues sit above all, 1 - Random Lane Change, 2 - Random Navigation and 3 - Fining Violators; if these three problems got solved, then traffic congestion will be sustainably nailed down, not only in New York City but also around the world.

1 - Random Lane Change
Traffic researchers around the world, have stated that changing lanes is the main reason that causes traffic congestion, it creates a sequence of events that slows everyone down.

2 - Random Navigation
Because the majority of roads are free to be used anytime, people use them as much as they like. People also are free to use GPS in changing their routes whenever they like, without any kind of discipline or precision which compturised other situations.

3 - Fining Violators
The current way of fining violators is not fitting the challenges of today's traffic jams. The governmental future visions to solve the traffic congestion hasn't come up with any great solutions to fine violators without having officers blocking the traffic.
